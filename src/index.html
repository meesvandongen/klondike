Solitaire Suite

Six classic card games in the Windows Vista style.

  • Klondike Build foundations A→K by suit. Guaranteed-winnable deals.
  • FreeCell 4 free cells; almost every deal is solvable.
  • Spider Spades only — clear eight K→A runs.
  • TriPeaks Chain consecutive ranks to clear three peaks.
  • Pyramid Pair cards summing to 13 to clear the pyramid.
  • Hearts Avoid hearts and the Queen of Spades. Lowest total wins.
